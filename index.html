Devoid of Demonetization

Background:

Your dream has always been to be a successful famous YouTuber. Although you're given the opportunity as your parents support you, the YouTube algorithm makes it difficult for you to achieve your dream.

How To Play:

You are the YouTube symbol on the left side of the screen.

Your objective is to make YouTube videos and to do so you must avoid the Demonetization blocks coming to you on the right side of the screen.

Use the space bar to jump up.

Use the "A" key to move left. Use the "D" key to move right.

You lose as soon as you hit the Demonetization blocks and get 1 point per Demonetization block dodged.

Press The Enter Key To Start
